About ISRO

Our Vision

At ISRO, our vision is to harness space technology for a wide range of applications, including communication, Earth observation, navigation, scientific research, and exploration. We strive to leverage our expertise to address the challenges facing our country and contribute to global progress.

Our Mission

Our mission is to design, develop, and deploy space systems that cater to various domains, such as satellite communication, Earth observation, navigation, space science, and planetary exploration. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of innovation and excellence in space technology, with a strong emphasis on indigenous development.

Key Objectives

  • Space Exploration: ISRO endeavors to explore celestial bodies, conduct space research, and advance our understanding of the universe.
  • Communication and Navigation: We have established a robust network of communication satellites that provide telecommunication, broadcasting, and broadband services across the country.
  • Earth Observation: ISRO's fleet of Earth observation satellites captures crucial data for monitoring agriculture, weather forecasting, disaster management, urban planning, and environmental assessment.
  • Space Research: Through space-based research, ISRO contributes to scientific advancements and innovation.

Our Achievements

Over the years, ISRO has achieved numerous milestones that have garnered global recognition. These include successful launches of satellites for various purposes, launching interplanetary missions, setting world records for launching multiple satellites in a single mission, and demonstrating cost-effective space solutions.

Commitment to Sustainability

ISRO is dedicated to sustainable practices in space technology development and operations. We strive to minimize our environmental impact while pursuing cutting-edge space endeavors.
